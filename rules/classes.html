Character Classes

Every adventurer has a class that defines their heroic capabilities. You-Meet-In-A-Tavern (YMIAT) compresses the Black Flag Reference Document 20-level progression into 10 meaningful levels, using Fitness, Insight, and Willpower instead of traditional six abilities.

Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level. Improvement (talents or ability increases) arrives at 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level. Casters use Spell Power and max spell circles per level instead of daily spell slots. Each class lists one save with advantage—the class's primary saving throw.

Class Features

Unarmored Defense

1st-level feature

While you aren't wearing armor, your base AC equals 10 + 2× your Fitness modifier. You can use a shield and still gain this benefit.

Rage

1st-level feature (free action)

On your turn, you can rage as a free action. While raging (and not wearing heavy armor): advantage on FIT checks and saves; bonus melee damage per Rage + column; resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing (attackers have disadvantage). You can't cast or concentrate on spells while raging. Rage lasts 1 minute or ends early as in BFRD. Uses per long rest per progression table.

Danger Sense

1st-level feature

You have advantage on Fitness saving throws unless you have the Incapacitated condition.

Reckless Attack

1st-level feature

When you attack on your turn, you can attack recklessly: advantage on melee FIT attacks this turn, but disadvantage on your Defense roll until the start of your next turn.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Increase one ability by 2, two abilities by 1 each, or one ability by 1 and choose a martial talent.

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Choose one: Instant Rage (rage without a free action when you roll initiative and use Fast Movement); Stubborn Rage (rage ends only when duration expires, you fall unconscious, or you choose to end it); Bloody Rage (while raging, slashing damage you deal with weapon attacks ignores resistance); Vengeful Rage (when damaged while raging, stacking advantage on your first attack against that creature next turn).

Relentless Rage

7th-level feature

While raging, if reduced to Max Wounds, make a DC 10 FIT save to avoid falling unconscious until rage ends. DC increases by 5 each use; resets on rest.

Epic Boon: Primal Champion

10th-level feature

Your Fitness modifier increases by 2 (maximum modifier now +7).

Class Features

Bardic Inspiration

1st-level feature (action)

Grant a d6 inspiration die to a creature within 60 feet (not yourself). Uses equal to WIL modifier per long rest. Die becomes d8 at 4th, d10 at 7th. Font of Inspiration (3rd): regain uses on short or long rest; reaction to add die to failed roll.

Bardic Performance

2nd-level feature

Performance effects as action; maintain with action each turn. Uses per PB per long rest. Celebrate Life heals allies; Cutting Words subtracts inspiration die from enemy rolls; Clarity of Thought (5th) grants charm/frighten protection.

Martial Action

1st-level feature (second action if no movement used)

Choose Aim, Guard, Quick Strike, or Wind Up. In YMIAT, use as your second action when you have not moved this turn (attacks at disadvantage if second action is an attack).

Action Surge

2nd-level feature

Gain another action on your turn once per short rest (twice at 7th, thrice at 10th; once per turn max).

Last Stand

1st-level feature (reaction)

When damage would bring you below half Max Wounds, spend hit dice (up to PB) to heal.

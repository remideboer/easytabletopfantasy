Backgrounds

Background helps define your character's personal history before taking up the mantle of an adventurer.

Backgrounds from the Tales of the Valiant Player's Guide (2024 core and 2026 options). In YMIAT, only skills explicitly granted benefit from proficiency advantage.

You have spent your life in service to a temple dedicated to a specific god or pantheon, acting as an intermediary between the divine and the mortal world—performing sacred rites, offering blessings, and conducting worshipers into the presence of the divine. You aren't necessarily a cleric; performing sacred rites isn't the same as channeling divine power.

Those who share your faith support you (but only you) at a modest lifestyle, and you can expect free healing and care at any temple, shrine, or established presence of your faith, though you must still provide any material components needed for spells.

Skill Proficiencies: Insight and Religion.

Additional Proficiencies: Calligrapher's supplies, and two languages of your choice.

Equipment: A holy symbol, a prayer book or prayer wheel, 5 sticks of incense, vestments, common clothes, and a pouch containing 15 gp.

Talent: Choose one: Field Medic, Mental Fortitude, or Ritualist.

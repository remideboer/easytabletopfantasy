Class Features

Pact Magic

1st-level feature

Few slots, short-rest recovery.

Pact spell slots per progression table (all slots are your slot level). Regain on short or long rest. Also cast using Spell Power per YMIAT rules. Spells known per table.

Eldritch Invocations

1st level and higher

Customize your pact with invocations.

Choose invocations from the BFRD Warlock list. Gain more at the levels shown on your progression table. Prerequisites apply.

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Warlock heroic boon per BFRD.

Choose a heroic boon from the BFRD Warlock list, adapted for YMIAT.

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Warlock epic boon per BFRD.

Gain your Warlock Epic Boon from BFRD, adapted for YMIAT.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a magic talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the magic talents list.

Fiend

Power from a greater fiend patron.

Dark One's Blessing

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fiend

Temp HP when foes fall nearby.

When you reduce a hostile creature to 0 Wounds, or one within 30 feet does, gain temporary Wounds equal to PB + warlock level until expended or long rest.

Fiend Pact Spells

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fiend

Always-known fiend pact spells.

These domain spells are always prepared:

  • Level 2: burning hands, command
  • Level 5: scorching ray, suggestion
  • Level 9: fireball, stinking cloud
  • Level 10: blight, wall of fire

Dark One's Own Luck

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fiend

Add 1d10 to a failed check or save.

When you make an ability check or save, add 1d10. Once per short or long rest.

Fiendish Resilience

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fiend

Resistance to a chosen damage type.

After a short or long rest, choose a damage type. You have resistance until you choose again.

Hurl Through Hell

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fiend

Banish attacker through a nightmare.

When you hit with an attack, the target disappears until end of your next turn, then returns (or nearest space). Non-fiends take 10d10 psychic damage. Once per long rest.

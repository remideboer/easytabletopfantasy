Class Features

Pact Magic

1st-level feature

Few slots, short-rest recovery.

Pact spell slots per progression table (all slots are your slot level). Regain on short or long rest. Also cast using Spell Power per YMIAT rules. Spells known per table.

Eldritch Invocations

1st level and higher

Customize your pact with invocations.

Choose invocations from the BFRD Warlock list. Gain more at the levels shown on your progression table. Prerequisites apply.

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Warlock heroic boon per BFRD.

Choose a heroic boon from the BFRD Warlock list, adapted for YMIAT.

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Warlock epic boon per BFRD.

Gain your Warlock Epic Boon from BFRD, adapted for YMIAT.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a magic talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the magic talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Archfey

Bargains with capricious fey lords.

Archfey Pact Spells

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Archfey

Always-known archfey pact spells.

These domain spells are always prepared:

  • Level 2: faerie fire, sleep
  • Level 5: calm emotions, phantasmal force
  • Level 9: blink, plant growth
  • Level 10: dominate beast, greater invisibility

Fey Presence

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: ArchfeyAction: action

Action: charm or frighten creatures near you.

As an action, each creature in a 10-foot cube originating from you makes a Willpower save or is charmed or frightened (your choice) until end of your next turn. Once per short or long rest.

Misty Escape

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: ArchfeyAction: reaction

Turn invisible and teleport when hit.

When you take damage, use your reaction to become invisible and teleport up to 60 feet to an unoccupied space you can see. Invisibility lasts until start of your next turn or until you attack or cast a spell. Once per short or long rest.

Beguiling Defenses

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Archfey

Immune to charm; charmer must save or be charmed by you.

You can't be charmed. When a creature tries to charm you, it makes a Willpower save; on a failure it is charmed by you for 1 minute or until it takes damage.

Dark Delirium

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: ArchfeyAction: action

Trap a foe in a fey illusion.

As an action, one creature within 60 feet makes a Willpower save or is charmed and incapacitated, perceiving itself in an idyllic fey realm. The effect ends after 1 minute, if you dismiss it (no action), if the target takes damage, or if someone uses an action to shake the target awake. Once per long rest.

Fiend

Power from a greater fiend patron.

Dark One's Blessing

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fiend

Temp HP when foes fall nearby.

When you reduce a hostile creature to 0 Wounds, or one within 30 feet does, gain temporary Wounds equal to PB + warlock level until expended or long rest.

Fiend Pact Spells

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fiend

Always-known fiend pact spells.

These domain spells are always prepared:

  • Level 2: burning hands, command
  • Level 5: scorching ray, suggestion
  • Level 9: fireball, stinking cloud
  • Level 10: blight, wall of fire

Dark One's Own Luck

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fiend

Add 1d10 to a failed check or save.

When you make an ability check or save, add 1d10. Once per short or long rest.

Fiendish Resilience

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fiend

Resistance to a chosen damage type.

After a short or long rest, choose a damage type. You have resistance until you choose again.

Hurl Through Hell

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fiend

Banish attacker through a nightmare.

When you hit with an attack, the target disappears until end of your next turn, then returns (or nearest space). Non-fiends take 10d10 psychic damage. Once per long rest.

Great Old One

Whispers from beyond the stars.

Great Old One Pact Spells

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Great Old One

Always-known GOO pact spells.

These domain spells are always prepared:

  • Level 2: dissonant whispers, hideous laughter
  • Level 5: detect thoughts, phantasmal force
  • Level 9: clairvoyance, sending
  • Level 10: dominate beast, evard's black tentacles

Awakened Mind

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Great Old One

Telepathy with creatures you can see.

You can communicate telepathically with any creature you can see within 30 feet. You don't need to share a language, but the target must understand at least one language.

Entropic Ward

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Great Old OneAction: reaction

Impose disadvantage on an attack against you.

When a creature makes an attack roll against you, use your reaction to impose disadvantage. If the attack misses, you have advantage on the next attack roll you make against that creature before end of your next turn. Once per short or long rest.

Thought Shield

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Great Old One

Psychic resistance; reflect psychic damage to attackers.

You have resistance to psychic damage. When a creature deals psychic damage to you, that creature takes the same amount of psychic damage.

Create Thrall

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Great Old OneAction: action

Permanently charm a humanoid (until broken).

As an action, one humanoid within 30 feet makes a Willpower save or is charmed by you until you use this feature again or until you die. The thrall obeys telepathic commands if you are on the same plane. The target repeats the save every time it takes damage, ending the effect on a success. Once per long rest.

Entropic Dispersal

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Archon

As a reaction when you take damage, you can disperse

As a reaction when you take damage, you can disperse some of that damage into a nearby creature. The damage you take is reduced by an amount up to your warlock level, and one creature you can see within 5 feet of you instead takes that amount of damage. If the target is unwilling, it can make a CON save against your spell save DC. On a success or if the target is immune to that type of damage, the target doesn’t take the damage, and the damage you take isn’t reduced.

Fey Presence

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fey Noble

Your patron’s power inures you to fey trickery and allows

Your patron’s power inures you to fey trickery and allows you to use some of it yourself. You are resistant to the charmed and unconscious conditions, and you have advantage on ability checks and saves to discern the truth of an illusion.

Death Ruse

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fey Noble

As a reaction when you take damage, you can fall prone

As a reaction when you take damage, you can fall prone and appear to have been reduced to 0 HP. Make a CHA (Deception) check. This becomes the DC for creatures making WIS (Insight or Medicine) checks to determine if you’re actually dead. If you attack a creature that hasn’t successfully determined that you aren’t dead or unconscious, you have advantage on your attack roll, and if you target such a creature with a spell, it has disadvantage on the save. Once you have gained this benefit against a creature or once a creature has seen through your ruse, that creature can’t be affected by this feature again for 1 hour.

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Fey Noble

The emotional frivolity of the fey has steeled you against

The emotional frivolity of the fey has steeled you against attempts at manipulating your emotions and made you better at influencing the emotions of others. You are immune to the charmed and frightened conditions, and you can use your Fey Presence feature a number of times equal to your PB, regaining all expended uses when you finish a short or long rest.

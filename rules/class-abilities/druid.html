Class Features

Druidic

1st-level feature

Secret language of druids.

You know Druidic. You can leave hidden messages in natural elements that only druids understand. Others spot the message with a successful Insight (Perception) check but cannot decipher it without magic.

Nature's Gift

1st-level feature
Action: action

Channel ambient energy to heal.

As an action, choose one creature within 5 feet (can be yourself). Roll a number of d4s equal to your proficiency bonus (minimum 2d4). That creature heals Wounds equal to the total. No effect on Constructs or Undead.

Uses per long rest equal to your proficiency bonus.

Spellcasting

1st-level feature

Prepared Primordial spells.

As a conduit for Primordial magic, you cast spells using Spell Power (SP). Spell cost equals spell circle. Your max spell circle by level is shown on the Druid progression table.

You prepare spells in advance. You can prepare a number of spells equal to your Insight (INS) modifier + your druid level (minimum one). Change your prepared list during a long rest.

Spellcasting ability: Insight (INS). Spell save DC and spell attack modifier use your proficiency bonus + Insight (INS) modifier.

Cantrips & rituals: As shown on your progression table. Rituals do not cost Spell Power when cast using the ritual rules in Magic.

Second action casting: If you cast a spell as your second action (because you did not move this turn), spell attacks are at disadvantage and targets have advantage on saves against that spell.

Wild Shape

2nd level and higher
Action: free action

Channel nature for beast forms and spell recovery.

You channel nature as a free action (Beast Form) or action (Draw Power). Uses per rest on your progression table.

  • Beast Form. Assume a known beast form. See Improved Beast Form for CR limits by level.
  • Draw Power. Recover one expended spell circle up to your proficiency bonus. Cannot use while transformed.

Improved Beast Form

3rd level and higher

Assume more powerful beast forms.

When you learn beast forms, maximum CR by level:

  • 3rd: CR ¼
  • 5th: CR ½
  • 7th: CR 1
  • 9th: CR 2

Nature's Grace

8th-level feature

Attunement sustains and protects you.

You cannot be magically aged. You do not need food or water. Your ability modifiers and Max Wounds cannot be lowered except by a wish.

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Rite of the Kingdom or Shaper.

Choose one:

  • Rite of the Kingdom. Communicate basic ideas with Beasts; advantage on checks to influence them.
  • Rite of the Shaper. When combat begins with no Wild Shape uses left, regain one use (once per long rest).

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Unlimited wild shape; effortless spellcasting.

Archdruid. Wild Shape (Beast Form) has unlimited uses. You can ignore verbal and somatic components of Primordial spells, and material components without a listed cost.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a magic talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the magic talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Leaf

Guardian of growing things and peaceful wild places.

Leaf Ring Spells

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Leaf

Always-prepared leaf ring spells.

These domain spells are always prepared:

  • Level 2: barkskin, entangle, goodberry, spike growth
  • Level 5: plant growth, speak with plants
  • Level 7: blight, freedom of movement
  • Level 9: greater hold, tree stride

Green Song

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: LeafAction: action

Speak with plants; inspect flora with an action.

As an action, focus on a nonmagical plant within 30 feet to learn if it is edible, poisonous, its typical environment, and its condition. Plant creatures understand your speech and you understand theirs.

Wild Shape: Sacred Grove

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: LeafAction: action

Wild Shape creates a magical pocket grove.

Expend Wild Shape as an action to create a 10-foot-radius sacred grove for 1 minute (one at a time). Choose one property:

  • Tangle Weed: difficult terrain; reaction to restrain movers (Fitness save vs spell DC).
  • Tree Line: disadvantage on attacks against creatures in the grove; reaction to intercept one attack against you on solid ground (grove destroyed).
  • Wildflowers: advantage vs charmed/frightened; bonus action to destroy grove and end one creature's conditions—allies on solid ground gain temp Wounds = PB.

Take Root

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: LeafAction: free action

Bonus action root yourself; spend hit dice for healing while rooted.

As a free action on solid ground, root in place (speed 0, can't be moved or knocked prone) until you end it. If rooted when your turn begins, spend one hit die to regain Wounds = roll + Fitness modifier.

Grove Warden

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Leaf

Sacred grove expands to 30 feet; choose who is immune to its effects.

Sacred Grove radius becomes 30 feet. You decide whether each ally (including yourself) is immune to any effects the grove imposes.

Heart of the Forest

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Leaf

Grove saves you at 0 Wounds; acorn resurrection if destroyed.

At 0 Wounds inside your sacred grove, the grove withers and you regain Wounds equal to your druid level (once per long rest). If your body is destroyed, an acorn remains; if undisturbed or planted, you gain a new body in 1d10 days with full Wounds.

Shifter

Master of beast forms and primal transformation.

Potent Forms

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Shifter

Higher-CR beast forms; speak while transformed.

Choose beast forms with CR up to 1 (scaling with level per Improved Beast Form). You use the creature's Max Wounds when you transform. You can speak languages you know in beast form but still can't cast spells.

Quick Shift

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: ShifterAction: free action

Beast Form as a free action.

When you use Beast Form, you can transform as a free action instead of an action.

Shifter Ring Spells

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Shifter

Always-prepared shifter ring spells.

These domain spells are always prepared:

  • Level 2: alter self, animal friendship, speak with animals
  • Level 5: conjure animals, water breathing
  • Level 7: giant insect, polymorph
  • Level 9: dominate, insect plague

Beast's Fury

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Shifter

Bonus natural attack after Attack action in beast form.

After you take the Attack action in beast form, you can use your second action (if you haven't moved) to make one natural weapon attack for 1d6 + PB damage. Melee damage in beast form counts as magical.

Elemental Infusion

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Shifter

Infuse beast form with air, earth, fire, or water power.

Expend two Wild Shape uses to infuse your beast form with an elemental power (Air, Earth, Fire, or Water), gaining movement, resistance, and a rechargeable action per BFRD Shifter. Alternatively, spend one use as a reaction to switch infusion while transformed.

Manifold Mind

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Shifter

Cast spells in beast form.

You can cast Primordial spells while in beast form, performing verbal and somatic components normally. Material components still apply.

Adapted from BFRD Shifter.

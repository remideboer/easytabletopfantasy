Class Features

Last Stand

1st-level feature
Action: reaction

Emergency healing when badly wounded.

When damage would bring your Wounds above half your Max Wounds, you can use your reaction to spend hit dice (up to your proficiency bonus). Roll them and heal Wounds equal to the total + your Fitness modifier.

Martial Action

1st-level feature

Tactical second action when you hold your ground.

Choose one martial action: Aim, Guard, Quick Strike, or Wind Up (see BFRD Fighter).

In YMIAT, use this as your second action when you have not moved this turn. If the second action is an attack, that attack is at disadvantage.

Action Surge

2nd, 7th, and 10th level

Gain an additional action on your turn.

On your turn, gain another action in addition to your normal action. Once per short rest at 2nd level; twice at 7th; three times at 10th (still only once per turn).

Multiattack

3rd, 6th, and 9th level

Multiple attacks per Attack action.

Make two attacks with the Attack action at 3rd level, three at 6th, and four at 9th.

In YMIAT, each successful weapon attack typically inflicts 1 Wound (2 on a critical hit).

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Defiant saves or unstoppable recovery.

Choose one:

  • Defiant. When you fail a save, succeed instead. Once per long rest (twice at 7th, three times at 9th).
  • Unstoppable. At the start of your turn, end one of: blinded, charmed, frightened, incapacitated, paralyzed, or stunned. Uses equal to proficiency bonus per long rest.

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Devastating blows that ignore defenses.

Once per turn when you hit with a weapon attack, deal extra damage equal to your Fitness or Insight score (your choice). The damage ignores resistance and immunity and cannot be reduced.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a martial talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the martial talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Spell Blade

Blend arcane magic with weapon mastery.

Spell Blade Subclass

2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level
Subclass: Spell Blade

Spell Blade subclass features.

Spell Blade grants partial spellcasting (see multiclass rules). Full YMIAT Spell Blade features are being drafted.

Weapon Master

Mastery of chosen weapons and tactical stunts.

Mastery

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Weapon Master

Master three weapon types; reroll damage or weapon-option damage.

Choose three simple or martial weapon types you have proficiency with. Once per turn when you roll damage with a mastered weapon, reroll the weapon damage dice and use either result. Once per turn with a mastered weapon option, you can roll damage even if the option normally wouldn't.

Stunts

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Weapon Master

Tactical maneuvers with weapon-specific requirements.

Stunt save DC = 8 + PB + Fitness modifier. Uses per short or long rest = PB + 1. One stunt per turn.

  • Arcing Strike (heavy melee): splash half damage to a second target in reach.
  • Cheap Shot (melee): extra unarmed strike on hit during Attack action.
  • Make It Count: single attack at +10; extra damage equal to fighter level.
  • Parry (melee, reaction): reduce damage by 1d10 + PB.
  • Redirect (reaction): reroll missed attack against adjacent target.
  • Riposte (melee, reaction): attack when enemy misses you.
  • Straight Through (ranged): splash half damage to adjacent target.
  • Tactical Retreat (melee): move half speed after hit without opportunity attacks.

Deadly Flourish

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Weapon Master

19–20 crits with mastered weapons; reaction attacks on crit.

Attacks with mastered weapons crit on 19–20. On a crit, use your reaction to either: make a ranged attack against up to PB creatures within 15 feet of the target, or make a separate melee attack against each creature within 5 feet of you.

Advanced Stunts

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Weapon Master

Powerful stunts available whenever you could use a stunt.

  • Assassin's Ambush: double damage dice from hidden.
  • Bulwark (shield, reaction): reduce spell damage by AC.
  • Felling Sweep (heavy melee, action): one attack roll vs. all in reach.
  • Preemptive Strike (melee, reaction): attack when foe enters reach.
  • Rapid Release (ranged/thrown): bonus attack after Attack action.
  • Wrestler's Clutch (melee): grapple on hit; stunts vs. grappled foe don't cost uses.

Grand Finale

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Weapon Master

Regain stunt uses when combat begins with none left.

When combat begins with no Stunt uses remaining, regain 3 uses. Once per long rest.

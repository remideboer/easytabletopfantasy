Class Features

Divine Sense

1st-level feature

Detect celestials, fiends, and undead.

On your turn, detect Celestials, Fiends, and Undead within 60 feet (not behind total cover) and consecrated/desecrated sites. Uses = proficiency bonus + 1 per long rest.

Lay on Hands

1st-level feature
Action: action

Pool of healing touch.

Pool = 5 × paladin level, restored on long rest. As an action, touch a creature and heal Wounds from the pool. Spend 5 healing to cure one disease or poison.

Divine Smite

2nd-level feature

Spend Spell Power for radiant burst on hit.

Once per turn when you hit with a melee weapon attack, spend Spell Power to deal extra radiant damage: 2d8 for 1st circle + 1d8 per higher circle (max 5d8). +1d8 vs Fiends and Undead.

Martial Action

2nd-level feature

Guard or Wind Up as second action.

Choose Guard or Wind Up. Use as your second action when you have not moved this turn (see Martial Action).

Spellcasting

2nd-level feature

Divine spells known (half caster).

As a conduit for Divine magic, you cast spells using Spell Power (SP). Spell cost equals spell circle. Your max spell circle by level is shown on the Paladin progression table.

You know a fixed list of spells and do not prepare them daily. Learn additional spells at the levels shown on your class progression table.

Spellcasting ability: Willpower (WIL). Spell save DC and spell attack modifier use your proficiency bonus + Willpower (WIL) modifier.

Cantrips & rituals: As shown on your progression table. Rituals do not cost Spell Power when cast using the ritual rules in Magic.

Second action casting: If you cast a spell as your second action (because you did not move this turn), spell attacks are at disadvantage and targets have advantage on saves against that spell.

Multiattack

3rd-level feature

Two attacks per Attack action.

Your physical prowess has grown. On your turn, you can make up to two attacks when you take the Attack action.

In YMIAT, each successful weapon attack typically inflicts 1 Wound (2 on a critical hit). Weapon bonuses apply to hit, not to the number of Wounds dealt.

Aura of Protection

4th-level feature

Bolster allies' saves.

When you or a friendly creature within 10 feet makes a save, they add your Willpower modifier (minimum +1). You must be conscious.

Aura of Courage

5th-level feature

Allies cannot be frightened.

You and friendly creatures within 10 feet cannot be frightened while you are conscious.

Channel Divinity

6th and 9th level

Oath-specific channel divinity.

Your oath grants Channel Divinity options. Uses: 1/rest until 6th level, then 2/rest, then 3/rest at 9th. Regain on short or long rest.

Cleansing Touch

7th-level feature

End spells on yourself or allies.

Action to end one spell on yourself or a willing creature you touch. Uses = Willpower modifier per long rest.

Aura Improvements

8th-level feature

Aura range increases to 30 feet.

Aura of Protection, Aura of Courage, and oath auras extend to 30 feet.

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Divine Recovery or Radiant Strikes.

Divine Recovery (Lay on Hands lets target expend a Recovery die) or Radiant Strikes (+1d8 radiant on weapon hits, 2d8 vs Fiends/Undead).

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Calming aura of salvation.

Action: 1-hour aura (30 ft). Allies resist nonmagical damage, auto-succeed death saves, and regain Willpower modifier Wounds at start of turn if they have any Wounds remaining. Once per long rest.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a martial talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the martial talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Devotion

Oath-bound knight of duty, honor, and righteous order.

Channel Divinity: Sacred Weapon

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: DevotionAction: action

Empower a held weapon with radiant light.

As an action, imbue a held weapon for 1 minute: add Willpower modifier to attack rolls (min +1); bright light 20 ft. Weapon becomes magical for the duration.

Channel Divinity: Sanctifying Light

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: DevotionAction: action

Blind aberrations, fey, fiends, and undead.

As an action, each Aberration, Fey, Fiend, or Undead within 30 feet that can see you makes a Willpower save or is blinded for 1 minute (Fitness save ends on self each turn).

Devotion Oath Spells

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Devotion

Always-known oath spells.

These domain spells are always prepared:

  • Level 2: sanctuary
  • Level 5: warding bond
  • Level 7: protection from energy
  • Level 9: stoneskin
  • Level 10: mass cure wounds

Aura of Devotion

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Devotion

Allies near you can't be charmed.

You and friendly creatures within 10 feet can't be charmed while you are conscious (30 feet at 8th paladin level via Aura Improvements).

Purity of Spirit

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Devotion

Protection from supernatural creatures.

Against Aberrations, Celestials, Elementals, Fey, Fiends, Undead, and Outsiders: they have disadvantage attacking you; you can't be charmed, frightened, or possessed; Divine Sense detects all listed types.

Holy Nimbus

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: DevotionAction: action

Radiant sunlight aura damages enemies.

As an action, emit sunlight (30 ft bright / 30 ft dim) for 1 minute. Enemies starting turn in bright light take radiant damage equal to paladin level. You have advantage on saves. Once per long rest.

Justice

Paladin of law who punishes the guilty without mercy.

Channel Divinity: Burden of Guilt

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: JusticeAction: free action

Curse a foe with halved speed and grounded flight.

As a free action, curse one foe you can see within 30 feet for 1 minute: speed halved; if flying at end of turn, Willpower save or fall. Ends if you fall unconscious.

Channel Divinity: Judgement

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: JusticeAction: free action

Mark a quarry for advantage and radiant damage.

As a free action, mark one target within 10 feet for 1 minute: advantage on attacks against it; weapon hits deal extra radiant = Willpower modifier. Ends if you damage another creature.

Justice Oath Spells

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Justice

Always-known justice oath spells.

These domain spells are always prepared:

  • Level 2: pendulum
  • Level 5: misty step
  • Level 9: haste
  • Level 10: greater invisibility

Aura of Vigilance

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Justice

Allies near you reposition when combat begins.

When combat begins, you and friendly creatures within 10 feet (30 feet at 8th paladin level) can each move up to 10 feet without provoking opportunity attacks before the first turn. You must not be incapacitated.

Unworthy Adversary

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: JusticeAction: reaction

Reaction intercept: move toward attacker and strike judged quarry.

When a creature within 30 feet attacks an ally, use your reaction to move up to half your speed toward the attacker. If the attacker is your Judgement target, make a weapon attack as part of the same reaction.

Divine Executioner

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: JusticeAction: action

Hour-long avatar of decisive justice.

As an action, channel order for 1 hour (once per long rest): reroll any damage you deal (must use new result); one extra attack when you take the Attack action; reaction when an enemy within 10 feet damages you or an ally forces that enemy to take half the damage dealt (same type).

Class Features

Martial Arts

1st-level feature

Unarmed strikes and monk weapons.

While unarmed or wielding only monk weapons without armor or shield:

  • Your unarmed strikes use the Martial Arts die from your progression table.
  • You use Fitness for attack and damage with unarmed strikes and monk weapons.
  • When you take the Attack action with an unarmed strike or monk weapon, you can make one unarmed strike as your second action if you have not moved (at disadvantage if it is an attack).

Unarmored Defense

1st-level feature

AC from agility and insight.

While unarmored and without a shield, AC = 10 + Fitness modifier + Insight modifier.

Techniques

2nd-level feature

Spend technique points on supernatural maneuvers.

Technique points per progression table, regained on short or long rest (30 min meditation). Options include Flurry of Blows, Patient Defense, Step of the Wind, and more. Save DC = 8 + PB + Insight modifier.

Unarmored Movement

2nd-level feature

Supernatural speed without armor.

Your speed increases while unarmored per the Movement column on your progression table.

Multiattack

3rd-level feature

Two attacks per Attack action.

Your physical prowess has grown. On your turn, you can make up to two attacks when you take the Attack action.

In YMIAT, each successful weapon attack typically inflicts 1 Wound (2 on a critical hit). Weapon bonuses apply to hit, not to the number of Wounds dealt.

Stunning Strike

3rd-level feature

Stun foes with ki-infused blows.

Once per turn when you hit with an unarmed strike or monk weapon, spend 1 technique point to force a Fitness save. On a failure, the target is stunned until the end of your next turn.

Empowered Strikes

4th-level feature

Unarmed strikes count as magical.

Your unarmed strikes count as magical for overcoming resistance and immunity to nonmagical attacks.

Evasion

4th-level feature

Dodge area effects.

When you make a Fitness save for half damage from an area effect, you take no damage on a success and half on a failure.

Perfect Motion

5th-level feature

Run on walls and fall safely.

Reduce falling damage by 5 × monk level as a reaction. Move along vertical surfaces and liquids without falling during the move.

Astral Teachings

7th-level feature

Temporarily master any skill.

Spend 2 technique points to gain proficiency in one language, skill, tool, or weapon until incapacitated or you use this again.

Diamond Soul

7th-level feature

Proficiency in all saves; reroll failures.

Proficiency in all saves. When you fail a save, spend 1 technique point to reroll (must take the new result).

Timeless Self

8th-level feature

Sustained by inner energy.

Cannot be magically aged; no food or water needed; ability modifiers and Max Wounds cannot be lowered except by wish.

Empty Body

9th-level feature

Invisibility and astral travel.

Spend 4 technique points to become invisible for 1 minute with resistance to all damage except force. Or spend 8 points to cast astral projection as an action.

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Purity of Body or Mind.

Choose Purity of Body (immune to disease/poison) or Purity of Mind (advantage on Insight saves; end charm/fear as free action).

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Technique points refill when combat begins.

When combat begins, regain up to 4 technique points. If you start a turn with 0 points, regain 2.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a martial talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the martial talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Flickering Dark

Harness shadow energy between strikes.

Dark Flame

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Flickering DarkAction: free action

Bonus action shadow aura: clawing, grasp, or spreading darkness.

As a free action, spend 2 technique points for a 10-foot-radius magical darkness (moves with you, 1 minute, not dispelled by spells of 5th circle or lower). Choose one property:

  • Clawing Shadows: creatures entering or starting turn inside take necrotic = martial arts die; you regain Wounds equal to necrotic dealt (Constructs/Undead unaffected).
  • Grasp of Nothingness: unarmed strikes deal extra necrotic = PB.
  • Spreading Darkness: action + 1 technique point to cast darkness without a slot.

At 6th level, choose two properties instead of one.

Obsidian Eyes

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Flickering Dark

Grant darkvision through magical darkness; upgrades at 4th/6th.

After 1 minute of meditation (during a rest), grant a willing creature obsidian eyes until their next long rest: see in dim light, darkness, and magical darkness as bright light (60 ft). At 4th monk level they see invisible creatures; at 6th they can't be blinded.

Grace of Umbra

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Flickering Dark

Step of the Wind grants intangibility and shadow teleport.

When you use Step of the Wind, you become insubstantial until end of next turn—outside sunlight you and your gear can move through narrow openings. Spend +1 technique point to teleport up to your speed to dim light, darkness, or magical darkness you can see. At 8th level, resistance to all damage except radiant while active.

Feeding Dark

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Flickering DarkAction: reaction

Weaken attackers in your magical darkness; long-range shadow step.

Creatures starting turn in your magical darkness make a Fitness save or deal half damage with Fitness/Dexterity weapon attacks until end of turn. When a creature saves or fails, use a reaction to teleport up to 1 mile adjacent to it (once per long rest, or 3 technique points).

Strike of Searing Shadow

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Flickering DarkAction: reaction

Reaction finisher that pulls foes toward a dying enemy.

When you hit with a melee attack, use a reaction and spend 5 technique points. Target makes Fitness save: 3d10 fire + 3d10 necrotic (half on success). Creatures you choose within 30 feet make Fitness save vs technique DC or take 2d10 force and are pulled 20 feet toward the target. Reducing a creature to 0 Wounds with this leaves its shadow permanently on the surface; you gain temp Wounds = half its Max Wounds.

Open Hand

Push, trip, and shatter foes with perfected technique.

Focus Intent

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Open HandAction: reaction

Reaction: add martial arts die to ally or enemy roll.

When you or a creature within 5 feet makes an ability check or attack roll, use your reaction to roll your martial arts die and add or subtract it from the roll. Uses = Insight modifier per long rest.

Open Hand Technique

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Open Hand

Flurry of Blows imposes knockdown, push, or no reactions.

When you hit with a Flurry of Blows attack, impose one effect: target can't take reactions until your next turn; Insight save or knocked prone; Fitness save or pushed 15 feet.

Wholeness of Body

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Open Hand

Heal when combat begins.

When combat begins with at least 1 Wound remaining, regain Wounds equal to Fitness modifier + monk level.

Tranquil Soul

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Open HandAction: action

Long-duration sanctuary via technique points.

As an action, spend 3 technique points to cast sanctuary on yourself until your next short or long rest (or until it ends normally).

Quivering Palm

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Open HandAction: action

Store lethal vibrations in a struck foe.

As an action, make one unarmed strike. On a hit, spend 4 technique points to store vibrations for days equal to monk level. Later, use an action to end them: target makes Fitness save or takes force damage equal to 5 × monk level and is paralyzed; failure by 5+ is instant death. Cost increases by 2 per additional use until long rest.

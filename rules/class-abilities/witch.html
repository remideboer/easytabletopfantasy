Class Features

Hex

1st-level feature
Action: bonus action

Curse foes with a Hex die.

As a bonus action, give a creature within 60 feet a Hex die (d6; d8 at 5th, d10 at 9th). When the target makes an attack, check, or save, you can roll and subtract the die (expending it). Only one Hex die per creature.

Uses per long rest equal to your Willpower modifier (minimum 1).

Spellcasting

1st-level feature

Known Wyrd spells.

As a conduit for Wyrd magic, you cast spells using Spell Power (SP). Spell cost equals spell circle. Your max spell circle by level is shown on the Witch progression table.

You know a fixed list of spells and do not prepare them daily. Learn additional spells at the levels shown on your class progression table.

Spellcasting ability: Willpower (WIL). Spell save DC and spell attack modifier use your proficiency bonus + Willpower (WIL) modifier.

Cantrips & rituals: As shown on your progression table. Rituals do not cost Spell Power when cast using the ritual rules in Magic.

Second action casting: If you cast a spell as your second action (because you did not move this turn), spell attacks are at disadvantage and targets have advantage on saves against that spell.

Shadow Craft

2nd-level feature

Minor illusions and witch-light craft.

You weave shadow and wyrd into minor effects—hidden messages, obscuring mists, and coven fetishes. Options expand at 6th and 9th level per the ToV witch rules.

Spirit Binding

2nd-level feature

Bind helpful spirits.

Over 10 minutes, bind a spirit companion for scouting and spell delivery. At 2nd level you maintain one binding; additional bindings at higher levels per ToV witch progression (adapted to YMIAT 10 levels).

Greater Hex

5th-level feature

Hexed foes struggle to hit you.

When you give a creature a Hex die, it has disadvantage on the first attack roll against you before the die is spent.

Otherworldly Form

9th-level feature
Action: action

Brief spirit transformation.

As an action, assume an otherworldly form for 1 minute: resistance to nonmagical B/P/S, advantage on Willpower saves, and one coven benefit from your subclass. Once per long rest.

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Witch heroic boon from ToV PG2.

Choose a witch heroic boon from the ToV Player's Guide 2 list, adapted for YMIAT.

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Witch epic boon from ToV PG2.

Choose a witch epic boon from the ToV Player's Guide 2 list, adapted for YMIAT.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a magic talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the magic talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Crimson Cord

Vitality witch who heals allies and wastes foes with blood magic.

Crimson Cord Coven Spells

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Crimson Cord

Always-prepared coven spells.

Gain coven spells at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level: cure wounds, inflict wounds, restoration, and higher-circle healing and necrotic spells per ToV Crimson Cord table.

Life Transfer

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Crimson CordAction: action

Sacrifice Wounds to heal or harm.

Action: sacrifice up to 4 × witch level Wounds to heal one creature within 30 feet or force a Fitness save for necrotic damage. PB uses per short or long rest.

Wasting Hex

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Crimson Cord

Hexes sap vitality.

When you Hex a creature, it may take 2 × PB Wounds at turn start (Fitness save for half) and can't regain Wounds until next turn.

Sanguine Link

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Crimson CordAction: action

Mystical blood bond.

Action: link with a willing creature (reduce Max Wounds by 10). Linked targets can receive Life Transfer and cure wounds at range. Half PB links active.

Scarlet Web

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Crimson CordAction: action

Area life-drain and healing aura.

Action: for 1 minute, hostiles in 30 feet take Wounds on entry/start of turn; allies ending turn nearby heal. Advantage on Fitness saves; resistance to nonmagical B/P/S. Once per long rest.

Adapted from BFRD Crimson Cord.

Night Song

Predator witch empowered by darkness and night blessings.

Night Blessing

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Night Song

Darkvision and nightly predator gifts.

Advantage on Stealth in dim light/darkness; darkvision 60 feet (+30 if you already have it). After each long rest, choose Night Children, Night Crawler, or Night Creature blessing.

Rending Hex

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Night Song

Hexed prey takes extra weapon damage.

When a hexed creature takes weapon damage, add Hex die + Willpower to damage without expending the die. Below half Wounds, Fitness save or allies gain advantage vs. target.

Draining Curse

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Night Song

Curse prey and drain their life.

Action or with Hex: sacrifice a Recovery Point to curse a creature for 1 hour; damage to cursed targets heals you up to witch level. PB uses per long rest.

Enhanced Blessing

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Night Song

Switch blessings on rest; stronger gifts.

Change Night Blessing on short or long rest. Each blessing gains an additional benefit (charm beasts, B/P/S resistance, or fly speed).

Apex Predator

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Night SongAction: action

Full night transformation.

Action: for 1 minute, gain two blessings, advantage vs. cursed targets, and regain 20 Wounds each turn outside sunlight. Once per long rest.

Adapted from BFRD Night Song.

Twilight Soul

Spirit diplomat with a sentinel companion.

Obscuring Hex

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Twilight Soul

Invisible to hexed targets.

When you Hex a creature, you are invisible to it for up to 1 minute until you attack, damage, or target it with a spell.

Spirit Sentinel

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Twilight Soul

Tiny spirit scout and spell origin.

10-minute ritual summons a Tiny spirit (bat, raven, cat, etc.). Cast spells from its space; telepathic senses; command Move, Hinder, or Invisible tasks.

Ephemeral Shroud

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Twilight SoulAction: reaction

Teleport away from attacks.

Reaction when hit by a single-target attack: teleport up to 60 feet; attack misses. PB uses per long rest.

Welcomed Spirits

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Twilight Soul

Ritual communion with spirits.

10-minute ceremony: expend Recovery Points to cast augury, speak with dead, or commune with nature without Spell Power.

Soul Sanctum

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Twilight SoulAction: action

Spirits shield you and punish foes.

Action: for 1 minute, hostiles near you or your sentinel take force damage at turn start; attackers have disadvantage vs. you; hostiles have disadvantage on saves vs. your spells. Once per long rest.

Adapted from BFRD Twilight Soul.

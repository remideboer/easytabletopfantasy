Class Features

Font of Magic

1st-level feature

Internal wellspring of sorcery points.

Sorcery points per progression table. Regain on long rest. Convert points ↔ spell levels per BFRD flexible casting (also use Spell Power in YMIAT).

Spellcasting

1st-level feature

Arcane spells known.

As a conduit for Arcane magic, you cast spells using Spell Power (SP). Spell cost equals spell level. Your max spell level by character level is shown on the Sorcerer progression table.

You know a fixed list of spells and do not prepare them daily. Learn additional spells at the levels shown on your class progression table.

Spellcasting ability: Willpower (WIL). Spell save DC and spell attack modifier use your proficiency bonus + Willpower (WIL) modifier.

Cantrips & rituals: As shown on your progression table. Rituals do not cost Spell Power when cast using the ritual rules in Magic.

Second action casting: If you cast a spell as your second action (because you did not move this turn), spell attacks are at disadvantage and targets have advantage on saves against that spell.

Metamagic

2nd level and higher

Twist spells with sorcery points.

Choose Metamagic options (Careful, Quickened, Twinned, Heightened, etc.). Gain more options at 4th, 8th, and 10th level. One option per spell unless noted.

Quickened Spell still costs 2 points; the spell uses your action (not a separate bonus action in YMIAT).

Sorcerous Renewal

3rd level and higher

Recover sorcery points on short rest.

After a short rest, roll 1d4+1 sorcery points (2d4+1 at 6th, 3d4+1 at 9th).

Devour Spell

7th-level feature

Absorb hostile spell energy.

Reaction when targeted by a spell: Willpower check DC 10 + spell level. On success, targets have advantage on saves and half damage; you gain sorcery points equal to the spell level. Once per long rest (or spend 4 points to reuse).

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Arcane Instruction or Innate Spell.

Arcane Instruction: spend 1 point to add Willpower to failed check. Innate Spell: learn a 1st–2nd level spell castable with sorcery points.

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Share spell effects with another target.

Reaction when a spell affects you: a creature you see within 120 feet is also affected. Once per rest (or spend 10 sorcery points).

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a magic talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the magic talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Chaos

Wild surges of uncontrolled arcane power.

Chaos Manifestation

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Chaos

Random surge on crit or dropping foes with spell attacks.

When you reduce creatures to 0 Wounds or score a critical hit with a spell attack, roll d6 on the Chaos Manifestation table (invisibility, force burst, AC bonus, phasing, magical darkness, or teleport-with-risk). Once per turn.

Chaos Origin Spells

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Chaos

Always-known chaos origin spells.

These domain spells are always prepared:

  • Level 2: misty step, shatter
  • Level 5: blink, hypnotic pattern
  • Level 7: compulsion, polymorph
  • Level 9: seeming, telekinesis
  • Level 10: eyebite, irresistible dance

Volatile Magic

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Chaos

Leveled spells may trigger extra volatile effects.

When you cast a spell of 1st level or higher, roll d20 vs volatile DC (20 − sorcerer level + spell level). On a failure the spell still resolves but you also roll on the Volatile Spell Effect table (see Player's Guide). At 0 Luck, roll twice and take the higher result.

Embrace Chaos

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: ChaosAction: free action

Bonus action: suffer a volatile effect for sorcery points or temp Wounds.

As a free action, roll on the Volatile Spell Effect table and suffer it as if targeted. Even result = gain PB sorcery points; odd result = gain temp Wounds = Fitness score for 1 hour. Once per short or long rest.

Inflict Disorder

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: ChaosAction: reaction

Redirect volatile effects onto attackers.

When targeted by a spell or attack, use your reaction to roll on the Volatile Spell Effect table; spend 1 sorcery point to force the attacker to suffer the effect instead. Once per long rest, or spend 2 sorcery points to reuse.

Apotheosis of Discord

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Chaos

6th-level+ spells trigger a minute of chaotic mastery.

When you cast a spell of 6th level or higher, become a conduit for 1 minute (until unconscious): metamagic costs −1 (min 0); resistance to all damage until end of next turn after casting a leveled spell; cast any known Arcane or origin spell (≤1 minute casting, spell level ≤ half PB) without slots or components. Once per long rest, or spend 15 sorcery points.

Draconic

Innate power from draconic ancestry.

Draconic Resilience

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Draconic

Natural AC and extra Max Wounds.

While not wearing armor: AC = 10 + Fitness modifier + Willpower modifier. Max Wounds increase by 1 per sorcerer level.

Dragon Ancestor

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Draconic

Draconic language and affinity; origin spells by dragon type.

Choose a dragon type (sets damage type for later features). Speak, read, and write Draconic. On dragon-related ability checks, treat d20 rolls below your sorcerer level as equal to your sorcerer level.

You learn additional origin spells at 2nd, 5th, 7th, and 9th level per BFRD Draconic Origin table (adapted to YMIAT spell levels).

Elemental Affinity

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Draconic

Add Willpower modifier to one damage roll of your ancestry type per cast.

When you cast a spell that deals damage of your ancestry type, add your Willpower modifier to one damage roll of that spell.

Imperious Wings

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: DraconicAction: free action

Sprout wings for fly speed.

As a free action, sprout wings for fly speed equal to walking speed until dismissed. Creatures that see you have disadvantage avoiding frighten/intimidate. Can't use while wearing armor.

Draconic Vengeance

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: DraconicAction: action

Brand a foe vulnerable to your element.

Action: target within 60 feet makes Willpower save or becomes vulnerable to your ancestry damage type until end of your next turn (immunity unchanged). Once per long rest, or spend 4 sorcery points to reuse.

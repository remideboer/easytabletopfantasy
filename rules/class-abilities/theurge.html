Class Features

Spell Nexus

1st-level feature
Action: bonus action

Swap prepared spells through your libram.

As a bonus action while your libram is within 100 feet, open a conduit to instantly replace one prepared spell with another of the same circle and source (Arcane or Divine) transcribed in your libram.

Uses per long rest equal to your proficiency bonus.

Spellcasting

1st-level feature

Prepared Arcane and Divine spells from your libram.

As a conduit for Arcane and Divine magic, you cast spells using Spell Power (SP). Spell cost equals spell circle. Your max spell circle by level is shown on the Theurge progression table.

You prepare spells in advance. You can prepare a number of spells equal to your Insight (INS) modifier + your theurge level (minimum one). Change your prepared list during a long rest.

Spellcasting ability: Insight (INS). Spell save DC and spell attack modifier use your proficiency bonus + Insight (INS) modifier.

Cantrips & rituals: As shown on your progression table. Rituals do not cost Spell Power when cast using the ritual rules in Magic.

Second action casting: If you cast a spell as your second action (because you did not move this turn), spell attacks are at disadvantage and targets have advantage on saves against that spell.

Libram: Your Tiny magical libram holds all spells you know except cantrips. At 1st level it contains six 1st-circle spells (at least two Arcane and two Divine). Only you can read it. Replace a lost libram with a 1-hour ceremony.

When you gain a theurge level, add one Arcane and one Divine spell to your libram. You may swap one spell in your libram for another of the same source when you level.

Spellcraft

2nd-level feature

Insight dice to manipulate spell outcomes.

You have Spellcraft dice equal to your Insight modifier (minimum 1). They are d6s (d8 at 9th level). Regain expended dice on a short or long rest.

Spend one die (roll immediately) to:

  • Add the result to damage or healing from a spell you cast.
  • Add the result to a Fitness save to maintain concentration.
  • Add the result to a check to learn, decipher, or recall magical information.

Summon Libram

2nd-level feature
Action: bonus action

Sense and recall your libram across planes.

While your libram is on the same plane, you know its distance and direction. As a bonus action on the same plane, summon it to your hand or an adjacent space.

Spell Synthesis

5th-level feature

Cast Arcane and Divine spells in one turn.

Once per short or long rest, cast two prepared spells of 4th circle or lower on your turn: one Arcane and one Divine. Use action + bonus action, or cast two action spells with one Cast a Spell action. Each spell costs Spell Power normally.

You cannot take reactions until the start of your next turn.

Superior Focus

6th-level feature

Dual concentration on Arcane and Divine spells.

Concentrate on one Arcane and one Divine spell at once. At the start of each turn, make a Fitness save (DC 10 + highest circle) or both spells end. You have disadvantage on concentration saves from damage while holding both.

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Careful Craft, Persistent Craft, or Precise Craft.

Choose one ToV theurge heroic boon: Careful Craft (Spellcraft die not expended on failed boost), Persistent Craft (force reroll on successful save vs. your spell), or Precise Craft (reroll 1s on Spellcraft dice).

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Epic theurge boon from ToV Player's Guide 2.

Choose an epic boon from the ToV theurge list (adapted for YMIAT), such as expanded Spell Synthesis uses or enhanced libram range.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a magic talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the magic talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Conduit

Exchange vitality for spell power and disrupt enemy magic.

Energy Exchange

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: ConduitAction: bonus action / action

Trade Recovery Points or spell circles for spell recovery or item charges.

Exchange Life: Bonus action: expend Recovery Points equal to a spell circle to recover that circle (lose Wounds equal to rolls + Fitness modifier; reduce Max Wounds until long rest).

Exchange Power: Action: expend a spell circle to restore charges to a touched magic item.

Mystic Vitality

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Conduit

Unspent spell circles become Recovery Points on rest.

After a long rest, regain extra Recovery Points equal to half your unspent spell circles (rounded down), up to your maximum.

Metabolize

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: ConduitAction: reaction

Store incoming energy to upcast a spell.

Reaction when you take non-B/P/S damage: Fitness save (DC 10 or half damage). On success, store energy up to 1 minute; expend it to cast a spell one circle higher. PB uses per long rest.

Spell Surge

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Conduit

Expend Recovery Points to add spell damage.

When your Arcane or Divine spell damages a creature, expend Recovery Points and add Fitness modifier; one target takes extra damage of the spell's type.

Nullify

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: ConduitAction: action

Suppress a creature's or item's magic.

Action: suppress magic on one creature or item within 120 feet for 1 minute. Creatures must pass Fitness saves to cast or attack with magic. Items function as nonmagical. Once per long rest.

Adapted from BFRD Conduit.

Illuminary

Inscribe sacred signs and sigils to extend your magic.

Mystic Scribe

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Illuminary

Learn languages and artist tools mastery.

Gain artist's tools proficiency (double PB if proficient). Over 10 minutes, temporarily learn a language until you use this again.

Sacred Sign

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: IlluminaryAction: bonus action

Mark allies to cast touch spells at range.

Bonus action: inscribe your symbol on a willing creature for 10 minutes. While active, cast touch spells on them within 120 feet; action to see through their senses.

Active symbols: 1 at 2nd level, 2 at 4th, 3 at 6th, 4 at 8th.

Spell Sigil

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: IlluminaryAction: action

Store spells in inscribed items.

Action: inscribe a spell into a worn/wielded item for 24 hours. You or a designated ally activates it with an action. Half PB sigils active at once.

Guarding Glyph

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Illuminary

Marked allies share defensive bonuses.

When you mark a creature with Sacred Sign, you gain a reciprocal mark. You and marked allies within 30 feet gain +1 AC and saves per marked ally nearby.

Potent Symbol

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: IlluminaryAction: action

Cast symbol without cost.

Action: cast symbol without Spell Power or components. Once per long rest.

Adapted from BFRD Illuminary.

Source Spinner

Unravel and realign the weave of magic.

Magic Theory

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Source Spinner

Bonus Spellcraft on magic knowledge; extra prepared spells.

Add a Spellcraft die to magic knowledge checks without expending it. Gain additional always-prepared spells (detect magic, perceive vulnerability, and higher-circle spells as you level).

Overwrite

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Source Spinner

End a spell when you cast on the same target.

When you cast a spell on a creature, attempt to end one ongoing spell on it (auto if equal/lower circle; otherwise Insight check vs. 10 + circle).

Mystic Realignment

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Source SpinnerAction: bonus action

Trade spell circles for recovered circles.

Bonus action: exchange spell circles for recovered circles of equal or lesser total value (see ToV Source Spinner rules).

Unravel

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Source SpinnerAction: reaction

Turn failed saves into successes.

Reaction: when you or an ally within 30 feet fails a save vs. magic, cause success instead; source takes force damage equal to your theurge level. PB uses per long rest.

Spell Loom

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Source SpinnerAction: action

Manipulate magic in a small area.

Action: for 1 minute, heal when casting spells, redirect single-target spells that miss or you save against, and gain temp Wounds. Once per short or long rest.

Adapted from BFRD Source Spinner.

Class Features

Eyes of the Maker

1st-level feature

Identify magic items by touch.

When you touch a magic item, you learn its properties, attunement requirements, charges, active spells, and the spell that created it (if any).

Shard of Creation

1st-level feature

Magical tool with versatile charges.

You craft a Tiny shard of creation (charges = Insight modifier, minimum 1, regained on long rest). Properties include Inspire, Mend, and other BFRD options. The shard vanishes if you die or if apart from you for 24 hours; recreate with a 1-hour ritual.

Augment

2nd-level feature

Apply augment effects to carried items.

You apply augment effects to items you carry. Augment effects known and augmented item limits are on your progression table. See BFRD Mechanist augment list.

Efficient Action

2nd-level feature

Augment without wasting effort.

When you use your action to apply an augment effect, you can apply a second effect to a different item as part of the same action.

Multiattack

3rd-level feature

Two attacks per Attack action.

Your physical prowess has grown. On your turn, you can make up to two attacks when you take the Attack action.

In YMIAT, each successful weapon attack typically inflicts 1 Wound (2 on a critical hit). Weapon bonuses apply to hit, not to the number of Wounds dealt.

Rapid Augment

4th-level feature
Action: free action

Augment as a free action.

Once per turn, you can apply an augment effect as a free action instead of an action.

Greater Creation

5th-level feature

Craft more potent items.

You can create uncommon magic items using your shard and augment abilities per BFRD Greater Creation rules.

Engineer's Insight

6th-level feature

Flash of mechanical genius.

When you fail an Insight check with a tool you are proficient in, you can reroll and take the higher result. Once per short or long rest.

Ranged Augment

7th-level feature

Apply augments at range.

You can apply augment effects to items you can see within 30 feet.

Always Prepared

8th-level feature

Augments persist through rest.

Augment effects you apply remain until you dismiss them (no action) or apply a new effect to that item.

Perfect Creation

9th-level feature

Master-crafted magical items.

You can create rare magic items per BFRD Perfect Creation rules.

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Artificer heroic options per BFRD.

Choose a heroic boon from the BFRD Mechanist list, adapted for YMIAT.

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Capstone invention.

Gain the Mechanist Epic Boon from BFRD, adapted for YMIAT.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or utility/martial talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the utility or martial talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Armorer

Mystic metal armor and absorbing augments (BFRD Metallurgist).

Augment: Absorbing

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Armorer

Armor/shield absorbs elemental damage and grants resistance.

Unique augment (doesn't count against known augments): when you take acid, cold, fire, force, lightning, poison, or thunder damage while wearing/wielding the item, use your reaction to reduce damage by 2 × Insight modifier and gain resistance to that type for 1 minute. PB uses per long rest.

Mystic Metal

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Armorer

Transmute armor into wearable mystic metal suit.

After 1 hour of focus, transmute a nonmagical suit of armor into mystic metal (AC 13 + Insight modifier; gauntlets 1d6 + FIT or INS; summon as free action within 5 feet). Reverts if another creature dons it.

Heavy Hitter

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Armorer

Extra force damage and improved crit range in mystic metal.

While wearing mystic metal: weapon attacks deal 1d6 force (1d8 at 5th artificer level, 1d10 at 10th); crit on 19–20.

Juggernaut

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Armorer

Athletics advantage; charge grants attack advantage.

While in mystic metal: Athletics proficiency (or advantage if proficient); advantage on grapple/shove checks; if you move 15+ feet before a melee attack, advantage on that attack.

Full Metal

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: ArmorerAction: free action

Brief immunity to physical damage.

As a free action, enter Full Metal for 1 minute: immune to B/P/S; resistance to all other damage. Auto-activates at 0 Wounds once per long rest.

Adapted from BFRD Armorer.

Alchemist

Brew volatile concoctions and supportive elixirs.

Experimental Elixir

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Alchemist

Long-rest elixir for yourself or an ally.

When you finish a long rest, create one experimental elixir in a vial. As a bonus action, a creature can drink it (or you can throw it 20 feet; shatter on impact). Roll d6 for the effect:

  • 1–2 Healing: regain 2d4 + Insight Wounds.
  • 3 Swiftness: +10 ft speed for 1 hour.
  • 4 Resilience: +1 AC for 10 minutes.
  • 5 Flight: fly speed 10 ft for 10 minutes.
  • 6 Transformation: alter self for 10 minutes (Insight as spellcasting ability).

Unexpended elixirs lose potency after 24 hours. PB elixirs per long rest at 8th level.

Alchemical Savant

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Alchemist

Add Insight to alchemist-spell damage and healing.

When you cast an artificer spell that restores Wounds or deals acid, fire, necrotic, or poison damage, add your Insight modifier to one Wound restoration or damage roll.

Restorative Reagents

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Alchemist

Free healing when casting certain spells.

When you cast an artificer spell of 1st level or higher using a slot, the target (or you if self-targeted) regains Wounds equal to 2d6 + Insight modifier.

Chemical Mastery

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: AlchemistAction: action

Resistance to acid/poison; greater restoration on demand.

You have resistance to acid and poison damage and are immune to the poisoned condition. As an action, touch a creature and end one of: poisoned, paralyzed, or petrified (once per long rest).

Artillerist

Deploy ranged magical ordnance.

Arcane Grenades

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Artillerist

Throw force grenades; damage scales 1d8→4d8.

As an action (Use an Object) or by replacing one Attack action attack, throw an arcane grenade (range 20/60, proficient). Use Dexterity or Insight for attack; hit = force damage d8 + Insight (2d8 at 4th, 3d8 at 6th, 4d8 at 8th). One grenade per turn. PB modified grenades per short or long rest (dispersal, flash, kinetic, smoke—see Grenadier rules).

Augment: Explosive

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Artillerist

Unique augment: trap objects with force bursts.

Unique augment (doesn't count against known augments): imbue a Medium or smaller stationary item to explode on your trigger (10-ft sphere, DEX save vs augment DC, (PB+1)d6 force minimum 3d6). PB triggers per long rest.

Controlled Blasts

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Artillerist

No close-range disadvantage; protect allies from your ordnance.

No disadvantage on ranged attacks (including grenades) from enemies within 5 feet. When your items or grenades deal damage or impose conditions, choose up to PB allies to be immune. You are immune to your own grenades and augment explosions. Two grenades per turn.

Superior Firepower

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Artillerist

Turn misses into hits; extended range; disintegrating crits.

Once per turn when a grenade attack misses, turn it into a hit (Insight modifier uses per long rest). Grenade range 40/80. Crit that drops a foe to 0 Wounds disintegrates it (no magic items). Three grenades per turn.

Battle Smith

Field a steel defender companion.

Battle Ready

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Battle Smith

Insight for magic weapons; steel defender companion.

You can use Insight for attack and damage with magic weapons. You gain a steel defender (Medium Construct, PB + Insight AC, Wounds = 5 + five × artificer level). It acts after your turn, obeys commands, and makes force attacks (+ PB to hit, 1d8 + PB force). If reduced to 0 Wounds, revive after a long rest or 1-hour ritual.

Augment: Messenger

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Battle Smith

Augment items with disruptive noise or remote voice.

Unique augment: imbue a held/worn item with disruptive noise (WIS save or deafened 1 round; break concentration) or Master's Voice (speak/hear through item 1 minute). PB activations per long rest.

Arcane Jolt

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Battle Smith

Defender or you jolt a hit for extra force and advantage.

When you or your defender hits with a magic weapon, add 2d6 force damage. Once per turn when you cast an artificer spell, the next defender attack before your next turn has advantage.

Improved Defender

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Battle Smith

Defender gains Multiattack; jolt heals you.

Your steel defender makes two attacks when commanded to Attack. When you use Arcane Jolt, you or the defender regains Wounds equal to half the force damage dealt.

Auguring Device

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Cog Augur

You learn to transmute a small device into an augury tool

You learn to transmute a small device into an augury tool that aids you in calculating and predicting the future. If you spend 1 hour of uninterrupted focus in contact with an object small enough for you to hold in one hand (which can be done as part of a short or long rest), you can transmute that object into an auguring device. The chosen object must be a single object (though that object can have internal moving parts, like a watch or lock) and can’t be a set of tools. The transformation lasts until you choose to end it or until you use this feature to transmute a different object.

Intricate Predictions

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Cog Augur

You learn to upgrade your Auguring Device feature, which

You learn to upgrade your Auguring Device feature, which gains the following additional properties: • When you consult the device to learn a detail about a creature, you can now learn two pieces of information from that list. • When you use the weather prediction aspect of the device, you now have advantage on WIS (Survival) checks to predict the weather within the next 7 days. • While holding the device, you can spend 10 minutes communing with the device to learn details about current or future events or to learn the direction to a specific creature or object. These effects work like the divination or locate spells, except the device serves as the material component and isn’t consumed by the spell.

Improved Divining

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Cog Augur

You learn to adjust your Auguring Device feature’s

You learn to adjust your Auguring Device feature’s calculations to reduce its chances of failure. When you use the augury or divination aspects of the device, the chance of receiving a random answer or no answer, respectively, is a cumulative 10 percent instead of 25 percent. If the device is imbued with the Clairvoyant augment, you can choose for one of the targets to have advantage on all its attack rolls instead of only one.

Class Features

Manifestation of Faith

1st-level feature

Holy warrior or miracle worker path.

Choose how you manifest your faith:

  • Manifest Might. Proficiency with heavy armor and one martial weapon. Once per turn when you hit with a weapon attack, deal extra radiant or necrotic damage equal to your proficiency bonus.
  • Manifest Miracles. Learn one extra cantrip from any source (counts as Divine). Add your proficiency bonus to damage dealt by your Divine cantrips.

Spellcasting

1st-level feature

Prepared Divine spells using Spell Power.

As a conduit for Divine magic, you cast spells using Spell Power (SP). Spell cost equals spell circle. Your max spell circle by level is shown on the Cleric progression table.

You prepare spells in advance. You can prepare a number of spells equal to your Insight (INS) modifier + your cleric level (minimum one). Change your prepared list during a long rest.

Spellcasting ability: Insight (INS). Spell save DC and spell attack modifier use your proficiency bonus + Insight (INS) modifier.

Cantrips & rituals: As shown on your progression table. Rituals do not cost Spell Power when cast using the ritual rules in Magic.

Second action casting: If you cast a spell as your second action (because you did not move this turn), spell attacks are at disadvantage and targets have advantage on saves against that spell.

Channel Divinity

2nd, 5th, and 9th level
Action: free action

Channel divine energy beyond spells.

You channel divine energy as a free action. You start with Turn the Profane. Uses: 1/rest at 2nd, 2/rest at 5th, 3/rest at 9th. Regain all uses on a short or long rest.

See Turn the Profane and Destroy the Profane.

Turn the Profane

2nd-level feature

Turn fiends and undead.

As an action, present your holy symbol. Each Fiend and Undead of your choice within 30 feet that can see or hear you makes an Insight save against your spell save DC. On a failure, the creature is turned for 1 minute or until it takes damage.

A turned creature must move away from you and can only Dash or Dodge on its turn.

Destroy the Profane

4th, 6th, 8th, and 10th level

Destroy turned creatures below a CR threshold.

When a Fiend or Undead fails its save against Turn the Profane, it is instantly destroyed if its CR is at or below:

  • 4th level: CR ½
  • 6th level: CR 1
  • 8th level: CR 2
  • 10th level: CR 4

Divine Intervention

7th-level feature

Call on your deity in dire need.

At the start of your turn, roll d20 + proficiency bonus. On a 19 or lower, your deity does not intervene. On 20+, your deity intervenes.

On intervention, use your action to cast any Divine spell (or domain spell) of casting time action without spending Spell Power. If the circle exceeds your max, make a spellcasting ability check DC 10 + circle.

After a successful intervention, you cannot use this feature again for 1 week (or until a long rest once you have Epic Boon: Divine Herald).

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Consecration or wrath gifts.

Choose one:

  • Gift of Consecration. Immune to disease, poison damage, and poisoned. If you die, your body is preserved as gentle repose for up to a year.
  • Gift of Wrath. Resistance to radiant or necrotic (your choice). When you cast a damaging spell, you can change its damage type to radiant or necrotic.

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Automatic divine intervention.

Divine Herald. Divine Intervention succeeds automatically. You no longer wait a week between uses, though you can still use it only once per long rest.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a magic talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the magic talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Life

Celebrate vitality and oppose the perversion of undeath.

Channel Divinity: Preserve Life

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: LifeAction: action

Channel divinity to distribute healing among nearby allies.

As an action, present your holy symbol and restore Wounds equal to 5 × your cleric level, divided among creatures within 30 feet. No creature can be restored above half its Max Wounds. No effect on Constructs or Undead.

Disciple of Life

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Life

Healing spells restore extra Wounds.

When you cast a Divine spell of 1st circle or higher to restore Wounds, the target regains 2 + the spell's circle additional Wounds.

Life Domain Spells

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Life

Always-prepared life domain spells.

These domain spells are always prepared:

  • Level 2: bless, cure wounds, gentle repose, restoration
  • Level 5: mass healing word, revivify
  • Level 7: death ward, guardian of faith
  • Level 9: greater restoration, mass cure wounds

Blessed Healer

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Life

Healing others heals you.

When you cast a Divine spell of 1st circle or higher that restores Wounds to another creature, you regain 2 + the spell's circle Wounds.

Greater Preservation

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Life

Expanded Preserve Life with condition cleansing.

Preserve Life affects creatures within 60 feet. When you use it, one target can also: cure all diseases; end blinded, deafened, paralyzed, or poisoned; or neutralize all poisons.

Perfect Healing

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Life

Healing spells restore maximum Wounds.

When you cast a Divine spell of 1st circle or higher that restores Wounds, you automatically restore the maximum possible Wounds (no dice rolling).

Light

Wield radiant fire and searing truth against darkness.

Light Subclass

2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level
Subclass: Light

Light subclass features.

Light domain rules are being adapted for YMIAT. See BFRD Light domain for reference.

War

Champion courage and decisive victory in battle.

Channel Divinity: Mark of Triumph

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: WarAction: free action

Mark a foe so allies gain advantage on the first attack against it.

As a free action, present your holy symbol and mark one creature within 60 feet you can see. Until the start of your next turn, the first attack roll each ally makes against the marked creature has advantage.

Disciple of War

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: War

Extra weapon attack when you hit with the Attack action.

Once per turn when you hit with a weapon attack during the Attack action, you can make one additional weapon attack as part of that same action. Uses per short or long rest equal to your PB.

Expanded Talent List

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: War

Martial or magic talents on Improvement.

When you gain a talent from Improvement, you can choose from the magic or martial talent lists.

War Domain Spells

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: War

Always-prepared war domain spells.

These domain spells are always prepared:

  • Level 2: command, magic weapon, shield of faith, warding bond
  • Level 5: fear, spirit guardians
  • Level 7: death ward, guardian of faith
  • Level 9: antilife shell, flame strike

Blessed Warrior

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: War

Martial weapon proficiency and Fitness-based attacks.

You gain proficiency with martial weapons. You can use Fitness for attack and damage rolls with martial weapons.

Greater Triumph

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: War

Marked creatures have disadvantage on saves against your spells.

Creatures marked by Mark of Triumph have disadvantage on saves against your Divine spells until the mark ends.

Holy Strike

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: War

Weapon attacks deal extra radiant damage once per turn.

Once per turn when you hit with a weapon attack, the attack deals 1d8 radiant damage in addition to the weapon's damage.

Class Features

Born Leader

1st-level feature

Authority or Community leadership aura.

Choose a leadership quality at 1st level:

  • Authority. A friendly creature starting its turn within 5 feet adds your PB to weapon damage on hits until end of turn. Gain Intimidation proficiency (double PB if already proficient).
  • Community. A friendly creature ending its turn within 5 feet gains PB on its first save before your next turn. Gain Persuasion proficiency (double PB if already proficient).

Allies benefit only while you are conscious.

War Banner

1st-level feature
Action: action

Mystical battle standard in combat.

As an action during encounter play, plant a war banner in an adjacent space. It lasts 1 minute (or until you end it, plant another, move 120+ feet away, or become incapacitated).

Creatures within 15 feet who can see the banner are affected. Start with Banner of Mercy (banner absorbs ally damage up to 10 × vanguard level Wounds) and Black Flag (foes have disadvantage on first attack each turn).

PB uses per long rest; only one banner at a time.

Stratagems

2nd-level feature

Tactical commands on the battlefield.

You learn stratagems—commands that reposition allies, grant advantage, or disrupt enemies. At 2nd level you know two; learn more as you level. Superior Stratagems (5th) unlocks advanced options from the ToV vanguard list.

Support Action

2nd-level feature

Stabilize and rally fallen allies.

You gain a Support Action each turn in combat. Use it to stabilize a dying ally (they regain Wounds equal to vanguard level + Willpower modifier and become conscious), grant temporary Wounds, or execute other support options from the ToV vanguard rules.

Multiattack

3rd-level feature

Two attacks with the Attack action.

Your physical prowess has grown. On your turn, you can make up to two attacks when you take the Attack action.

In YMIAT, each successful weapon attack typically inflicts 1 Wound (2 on a critical hit). Weapon bonuses apply to hit, not to the number of Wounds dealt.

War Banner: Lucky Colors

6th-level feature

Third war banner option.

Gain the Lucky Colors war banner: allies within range gain a bonus to Defense or saves (per ToV vanguard rules).

Manifold Tactic

7th-level feature

Combine two stratagems.

When you use a stratagem, you can apply a second stratagem effect on the same turn once per short or long rest.

Valiant Commander

9th-level feature

Extend banner and stratagem range.

Your war banner range increases to 30 feet. Once per turn when you use a stratagem, you can affect one additional valid target.

Battlefield Resolve

9th-level feature

Push through pain on the front line.

When you fail a save, you can expend a Recovery Point to reroll. You must use the new result.

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Vanguard heroic boon from ToV PG2.

Choose a vanguard heroic boon from the ToV Player's Guide 2 list, adapted for YMIAT.

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Vanguard epic boon from ToV PG2.

Choose a vanguard epic boon from the ToV Player's Guide 2 list, adapted for YMIAT.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a martial talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the martial talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Bulwark

Battlefield healer and protector with the Surgeon's Standard banner.

Battlefield Healer

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: BulwarkAction: bonus action

Fast stabilization and Medicine mastery.

Medicine proficiency (double PB if proficient). Stabilize dying creatures as a bonus action; they regain Wounds equal to vanguard level + Willpower modifier.

War Banner: Surgeon's Standard

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Bulwark

Healing war banner.

War banner option: at start of your turns, heal PB allies within range for 1d4 + Willpower (scales to 4d4 at 8th level).

Safeguard

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Bulwark

Protect nearby allies.

Bonus action: +2 AC for you and allies within 5 feet until next turn; reaction to redirect single-target attacks/spells to you. PB uses per rest.

Turn the Tide

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: BulwarkAction: action

Mass healing rally.

Action: expend vanguard Recovery Points; you and allies within 30 feet regain Wounds equal to total + Willpower modifier.

Bastion of Defense

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Bulwark

Enhanced safeguard stance.

While Safeguard is active: temp Wounds when activating; advantage and resistance vs. area damage for nearby allies.

Adapted from BFRD Bulwark.

Herald

Living standard-bearer who teleports allies through battle.

Color Guard

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Herald

Move banners and extend stratagem range.

Start of turn: move an active banner up to 30 feet. Allies need not see the banner; stratagems need not be seen or heard.

War Banner: Stirring Sign

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Herald

Speed and extra attack banner.

Allies starting turn in banner range double speed, gain advantage on Fitness saves, and can make one extra weapon attack when they Attack.

Regroup

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Herald

Teleport allies within banner range.

Bonus action: teleport yourself or a willing ally within banner radius. Reaction: teleport a damaged ally into the radius.

Double Standard

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Herald

Two banner effects at once.

When you plant a war banner, choose two active effects instead of one.

Flag Bearer

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Herald

Unlimited banners and emergency recovery.

War Banner unlimited uses; banners persist if you are incapacitated. End a banner to regain Wounds (level × Willpower) when below half Max Wounds. Once per long rest.

Adapted from BFRD Herald.

Marshal

Inspiring commander who coordinates allied strikes.

Inspiring Leadership

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Marshal

Grant advantage via stratagems.

When you use Stratagems, grant advantage on the next attack to 1 ally (2 at 4th, 3 at 6th, 4 at 8th) who can see and hear you.

War Banner: Banner of Coordination

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Marshal

Coordinated attack banner.

Allies in banner range gain +1 attack per other ally in range (max PB) until start of next turn.

General's Gambit

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Marshal

Spend Recovery Points to fix failed rolls.

When you or an ally within 30 feet fails a check or save, expend a Recovery Point and add the roll to the result.

Comrade in Arms

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Marshal

Share advantage and bonus damage.

When Inspiring Leadership grants advantage, you also gain advantage on your next attack. Hits with that advantage deal extra damage equal to PB + Willpower.

Battlefield Vigor

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Marshal

Regain Wounds when bloodied.

Start of turn below half Max Wounds (but above 0): regain Wounds equal to PB + Fitness modifier.

Adapted from BFRD Marshal.

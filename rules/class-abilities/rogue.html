Class Features

Sneak Attack

1st-level feature

Extra precision damage once per turn.

Once per turn, deal extra Sneak Attack dice when you hit with a finesse or ranged weapon if you have advantage, or if an ally is within 5 feet of the target. Damage per Sneak Attack column.

Expertise

1st and 4th level

Advantage on chosen skill checks.

Choose two proficiencies (four at 4th level) for advantage on related ability checks.

Thieves' Cant

1st-level feature

Secret criminal dialect.

You know Thieves' Cant for hidden messages and recognize criminal signs.

Cunning Action

2nd-level feature
Action: free action

Dash, Disengage, or Hide as a free action.

On your turn, use a free action to Dash, Disengage, or Hide.

Uncanny Dodge

3rd-level feature

Halve damage from one attack.

When an attacker you can see hits you, use your reaction to halve the Wounds from that attack.

Evasion

4th-level feature

Negate area damage on successful saves.

On successful Fitness saves vs area effects, take no damage; half on failure.

Reliable Talent

5th-level feature

Treat low rolls as 10 on proficient checks.

Treat d20 rolls of 9 or lower as 10 on ability checks with skills or tools you are proficient in.

Precise Critical

6th and 8th level

Critical hits on 19–20 with finesse/ranged.

Critical hit on 19–20 with finesse or ranged weapons. Roll 1 extra weapon die on crit at 6th level, 2 extra at 8th.

Keensense

7th-level feature

Hearing-based blindsight 10 feet.

Keensense 10 feet unless deafened.

Elusive

9th-level feature

Attackers rarely have advantage against you.

While not incapacitated, attack rolls cannot have advantage against you.

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Escape Artist or Jack of All Trades.

Escape Artist: area saves deal no damage on success. Jack of All Trades: pick talents from any list; gain one immediately.

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Turn failure into success.

Turn a missed attack into a hit, or treat a failed ability check d20 as 20. Once per short or long rest.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a technical talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the technical talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Enforcer

Intimidation, control, and ruthless precision.

Enforcer Subclass

2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level
Subclass: Enforcer

Enforcer subclass features.

YMIAT Enforcer rogue features are being drafted.

Thief

Climb, appraise, and strike before foes react.

Fast Hands

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Thief

Cunning Action can sleight of hand, use object, or pick locks.

Your Cunning Action free action can also: Sleight of Hand; Use an Object; or use thieves' tools to disarm a trap or open a lock.

Second-Story Work

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Thief

Climbing speed and improved jumps.

Climbing speed equals walking speed (+10 ft if you already had climbing speed). Long jump uses walking speed with 10 ft run-up; standing long jump half speed. May use Acrobatics instead of Athletics for jump checks.

Appraising Eye

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: ThiefAction: action

Identify value and magic of objects.

Action to inspect an object within 10 feet: learn if nonmagical (and value) or magical (1 minute study reveals type, rarity, curse, attunement; can ignore class requirements after study).

Trap Specialist

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Thief

Reaction disarm traps including magical ones.

When you or an ally within 5 feet triggers a trap, reaction to disarm it. Magical traps: ability check using Fitness modifier vs. trap DC.

Thief's Reflexes

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Thief

Two turns in the first round of combat.

Take two turns in round one: normal initiative and a second turn at initiative − 10. Can't use if surprised.

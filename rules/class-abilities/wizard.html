Class Features

Arcane Recovery

1st-level feature

Recover Spell Power on a short rest.

Once per day on a short rest, recover expended spell circles totaling half your wizard level (rounded up) as Spell Power, or per BFRD slot recovery adapted to SP.

Spellcasting

1st-level feature

Prepared Arcane spells from spellbook.

As a conduit for Arcane magic, you cast spells using Spell Power (SP). Spell cost equals spell circle. Your max spell circle by level is shown on the Wizard progression table.

You keep a spellbook. Prepare spells from it equal to your Insight (INS) modifier + your wizard level (minimum one). Add two spells to your spellbook each time you gain a level.

Spellcasting ability: Insight (INS). Spell save DC and spell attack modifier use your proficiency bonus + Insight (INS) modifier.

Cantrips & rituals: As shown on your progression table. Rituals do not cost Spell Power when cast using the ritual rules in Magic.

Second action casting: If you cast a spell as your second action (because you did not move this turn), spell attacks are at disadvantage and targets have advantage on saves against that spell.

Magic Sense

2nd-level feature

Detect magic within 30 feet.

Action: until end of next turn, sense spellcasters, ongoing spell effects, and magic items within 30 feet (blocked by 1 ft stone, 1 in metal, thin lead, 3 ft wood/dirt). Uses = PB + 1 per long rest.

Rote Spell

3rd level and higher

Always-prepared signature spells.

Choose one spell each at 3rd (1st circle), 5th (2nd), 7th (3rd), and 9th (4th) level as always-prepared rote spells. Swap during a long rest.

Superior Recovery

4th-level feature

Swap prepared spells during Arcane Recovery.

When you use Arcane Recovery, swap up to half your proficiency bonus (rounded down) prepared spells from your spellbook.

Spellguard

7th-level feature

Resist hostile magic.

Advantage on saves against spells; resistance to damage from spells and spell attacks.

Spell Mastery

9th-level feature

Cast rote spells without spending slots.

Cast each rote spell once per short rest at lowest circle without spending Spell Power.

Heroic Boon

5th-level feature

Rite of the Ritualist or Source Master.

Ritualist: learn rituals from any magic source. Source Master: add spells from Divine, Primordial, or Wyrd when leveling.

Epic Boon

10th-level feature

Chance to recover spent spell power.

When you cast a spell of 1st circle or higher using Spell Power, roll d10. If the result exceeds the circle spent, recover that Spell Power. Once per short rest.

Improvement

3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level

Ability increases or a magic talent.

At 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, and 10th level, choose one improvement (ability modifiers cannot exceed +7 with this feature):

  • Increase a single ability modifier by 2.
  • Increase two different ability modifiers by 1 each.
  • Increase one ability modifier by 1 and select a talent from the magic talents list.

Subclass Features

Choose your subclass at 2nd level. Subclass features arrive at 2nd, 4th, 6th, and 8th level.

Battle Mage

Arcane offense with martial discipline.

Expanded Talent List

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Battle Mage

Magic or martial talents on Improvement.

When you gain a talent from Improvement, choose from magic or martial talent lists.

Spell Ward

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Battle Mage

AC and physical resistance after casting.

When you cast an Arcane spell of 1st circle+, weave a ward for 1 minute: +PB AC and resistance to B/P/S (ends if unconscious or you end a turn without casting a leveled spell). PB uses per long rest.

Tactical Caster

2nd-level subclass feature
Subclass: Battle Mage

Allies immune to your damaging spells.

When you cast a damaging Arcane spell, choose yourself and allies you can see to be immune to that spell's damage. PB uses per long rest.

Contingency Plan

4th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Battle MageAction: reaction

Redirect missed spell attack to another target.

When a spell attack misses, use your reaction to redirect it to a different target in range with a new attack roll.

Blasting Power

6th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Battle Mage

Empowered spell damage after successful saves.

When a creature succeeds on a save against your Arcane spell but takes no damage, it takes force damage equal to spell circle d6 + Insight modifier.

Counterstrike

8th-level subclass feature
Subclass: Battle Mage

Punish creatures that save against your spells.

When a creature succeeds on a save against your Arcane spell and would suffer no effect, it takes force damage per Blasting Power. Once per turn.

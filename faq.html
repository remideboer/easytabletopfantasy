FAQ

Common questions about You-Meet-In-A-Tavern (YMIAT).

Is You-Meet-In-A-Tavern (YMIAT) Dungeons & Dragons?

No. You-Meet-In-A-Tavern (YMIAT) is a D&D 5e homebrew ruleset. It is based on the 5.1 System Reference Document but is not official Dungeons & Dragons and is not affiliated with Wizards of the Coast.

Is You-Meet-In-A-Tavern (YMIAT) free to use?

Yes. You-Meet-In-A-Tavern (YMIAT) is released under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License (CC BY 4.0) and is free to use, share, and adapt with proper attribution.

Can I create and publish content for You-Meet-In-A-Tavern (YMIAT)?

Yes. You may create, modify, and publish content based on You-Meet-In-A-Tavern (YMIAT) under the terms of the CC BY 4.0 license, as long as proper attribution is provided and trademarked names are respected.

What is You-Meet-In-A-Tavern (YMIAT) based on?

You-Meet-In-A-Tavern (YMIAT) is a homebrew based on the 5.1 System Reference Document and draws on ideas from Tales of the Valiant, Nimble, Pathfinder Second Edition, and other D&D-adjacent systems.

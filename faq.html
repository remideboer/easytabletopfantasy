FAQ

Common questions about Easy Tabletop Fantasy.

Is Easy Tabletop Fantasy Dungeons & Dragons?

No. Easy Tabletop Fantasy is an independent fantasy roleplaying game. It is inspired by open tabletop RPG design and the 5.1 System Reference Document, but it is not Dungeons & Dragons and is not affiliated with Wizards of the Coast.

Is Easy Tabletop Fantasy free to use?

Yes. Easy Tabletop Fantasy is released under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License (CC BY 4.0) and is free to use, share, and adapt with proper attribution.

Can I create and publish content for Easy Tabletop Fantasy?

Yes. You may create, modify, and publish content based on Easy Tabletop Fantasy under the terms of the CC BY 4.0 license, as long as proper attribution is provided and trademarked names are respected.

What games inspired Easy Tabletop Fantasy?

Easy Tabletop Fantasy is inspired by modern fantasy roleplaying games and design approaches, including the 5.1 System Reference Document, Tales of the Valiant, Nimble, Pathfinder Second Edition, and other systems that shaped the genre.
